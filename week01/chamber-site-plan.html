La Serena Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

La Serena Chamber of Commerce

This website is intended to represent the local chamber of La Serena, a city in the north of Chile. Its main focus is to promote and inform local entrepreneurs and businesses about everything related to business and commerce in the city, as well as the growth and business networking in the area.

Site Purpose

  • Inform entrepreneurs and businesses about the local economic situation.
  • Print reports about the most requested services and products within the area.
  • Help grow networking between businesses in the city and connect the right buyers with the right providers.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month to promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find information about starting a business in Springfield?
  • What are the membership benefits of joining the Springfield Chamber of Commerce?
